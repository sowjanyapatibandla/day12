Q 2 : Explain the difference between fixed and sticky

Fixed - The property fixed is relative to the viewport , when we apply position fixed property it gets fixed to that position so it doesn't move when we scroll the page up or down.
It doesn't takes the additional space of the page and doesn't disturb other elements.

Sticky - The propery Sticky is scrollable until we scroll the page till our specified position then as it reaches the specified position it becomes fixed at that position ex- is navbar.
It takes the space in the page and it will displace the other elements.
